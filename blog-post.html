BBC Interiors is proud to announce the successful completion of all electrical works at the newly constructed CP Hospital in Dhangadhi, Sudurpashchim Province. Our team meticulously installed and implemented the hospital's entire electrical infrastructure, ensuring a reliable and safe power supply for all medical equipment, patient care areas, and administrative facilities. This crucial contribution helps CP Hospital provide essential healthcare services to the community with confidence in its robust electrical systems.

Beyond a mere service contract

This goes beyond a mere service contract; it signifies a pivotal partnership. By delivering a flawless and dependable electrical backbone, BBC Interiors has empowered CP Hospital to operate effectively from day one, directly contributing to its capacity to provide high-quality patient care to the people of Dhangadhi and the surrounding region. Their professional execution ensured that a vital, complex component of the hospital's operational readiness was handled with supreme competence, allowing CP Hospital to fulfill its mission without compromise.
